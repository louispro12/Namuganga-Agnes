About Me

I am Namuganga Agnes, a professional herbalist and founder of St Anthony Herbal Centre based in Entebbe, Uganda. Born in 1982, I have dedicated my life to providing authentic herbal remedies and holistic wellness solutions.

Proud mother of five children and wife to Mr. Mulinde Richard, I balance family life with my professional work, empowering individuals and communities to embrace natural healing.

Through St Anthony Herbal Centre, I offer consultations, custom herbal blends, detox programs, and wellness workshops to promote a healthy lifestyle aligned with nature.
