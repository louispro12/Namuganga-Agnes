About Me

Hello! My name is Nawoova Gatrude, born in 2011. I am a lively and curious Primary 7 student at St. Savio Junior School, Kisubi. Every day at school is a new adventure filled with learning, fun, and laughter with friends and teachers.

Outside the classroom, I love exploring my creative side through dance, comedy skits, and participating in school activities. I believe that learning can be exciting when we mix it with joy and creativity. Sports are my way to stay active, healthy, and full of energy. Whether it’s running, football, or games with friends, I always try to give my best.

Life at home is filled with love and inspiration. Being the daughter of my amazing mother, Namuganga Agnes, who is a herbalist and entrepreneur, motivates me to dream big, work hard, and always be kind to others. I hope to grow up with the same dedication, creativity, and joy that I see in her every day.

Hobbies & Interests

My hobbies reflect my energetic and playful personality. I love dancing because it allows me to express my feelings and creativity. I also enjoy scrolling TikTok to discover new trends, learn fun dances, and gather inspiration for my own creative ideas. Playing sports keeps me active, helps me focus, and gives me a sense of teamwork and friendship.

My day usually starts with school, where I enjoy reading, solving problems, and interacting with classmates. After school, I spend time practicing dance routines or preparing fun skits. I believe that every day is an opportunity to learn, create, and share joy. Staying curious, positive, and active makes life exciting and fulfilling.

Family & Inspiration

I am inspired every day by my family, especially my mother, Namuganga Agnes. Seeing her dedication to helping others through herbal medicine encourages me to be kind, hardworking, and creative.
